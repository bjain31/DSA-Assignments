
Question 1

1.) Find the time complexity for the following scenarios

a.) for(i=1;i<=n;i++)
    for(j=i;i<=n;j++)
    printf("Hi");

b.) for(i=1;i<=n;i*=3)
    for(j=1;i<=n;j++)
    printf("Hello");

Answer 1

The above two scenarios are infinite loops. Infinite loop is executed infinite times. Therefore, there is no algorithm time complexity for infinite loops.
